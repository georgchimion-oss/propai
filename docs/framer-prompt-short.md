# Framer AI Prompt (Short Version)

Copy below this line:

---

Design a premium SaaS landing page for "Vestia" — an AI-powered property management platform for South Florida condo associations.

Brand: Premium, trustworthy. Think Compass real estate meets Stripe. NOT generic AI purple gradients.

Colors: Dark navy base (#07070c), warm gold accent (#c9a96e), teal secondary (#2dd4bf), off-white for light sections (#f8f7f4).

Typography: Elegant serif headlines (Cormorant Garamond), clean sans-serif body (Plus Jakarta Sans). Should feel like a luxury property brochure.

Photography: Use real photos throughout — Miami high-rises at dusk, condo lobbies, rooftop pools, property managers on tablets. Every section needs a visual anchor. No illustrations-only sections.

SECTIONS:

1. HERO — Full-bleed aerial photo of Miami Beach condos at golden hour. Dark gradient overlay. Badge: "AI-Powered Property Management" with pulsing dot. H1: "The future of property intelligence." Subtitle about six AI modules for condo associations. Two stat pills: "27/30 AI Disruption Score" and "38% automatable." CTAs: "See the Platform" (gold) + "Watch Demo" (outline).

2. PROBLEM — Split layout. Left: photo of overwhelmed property manager buried in papers. Right: "Property managers are drowning." Stats about email/phone coordination, $19.5B managed on spreadsheets, board members personally liable.

3. SIX MODULES — 3x2 card grid. Each card: colored SVG icon, name, description, feature tags. Modules: Vestia Triage (red, maintenance AI), Vestia Comply (teal, SB 4-D compliance), Vestia Collect (gold, rent collection), Vestia Screen (purple, tenant screening), Vestia Docs (blue, lease automation), Vestia Vendor (orange, vendor RFP/bids).

4. HOW IT WORKS — 3 horizontal steps with connecting lines. Report → AI Triage → Resolve. Subtle photo background of condo building.

5. MARKET STATS — Photo background: aerial Miami condo row, dark overlay. Four stat cards: $88B PropTech market, 49.8K FL associations, $19.5B annual assessments, 38% ripe for disruption. Headline: "South Florida is the perfect market."

6. COMPLIANCE — Full-width teal-accented card. Post-Surfside Building Safety Act (SB 4-D). Six checkmarks: inspections, reserve studies, 15-year retention, mandatory funding, board notifications, CTMH reporting. Emphasis: board members personally liable.

7. CTA — Photo background: condo rooftop pool at sunset. "Ready to modernize?" Email input + "Get Early Access" button.

8. FOOTER — Logo, nav links, "A Georg Chimion project," Miami FL.

Design rules: Alternate dark/photo backgrounds. Glass morphism on cards. Mobile-first (iPhone 15 Pro). Fade-up scroll animations. Parallax on photos. Gold for CTAs, teal for AI/compliance features.
