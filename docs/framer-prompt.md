---

Design a premium SaaS landing page for "Vestia" — an AI-powered property management platform targeting South Florida condo associations and HOAs.

## Brand
- **Name**: Vestia
- **Tagline**: "The future of property intelligence"
- **Tone**: Premium, trustworthy, sophisticated — like a fintech product meets luxury real estate
- **NOT generic AI purple gradients. NOT startup-y. Think: Compass real estate meets Stripe's dashboard meets a luxury condo marketing site.**

## Visual Direction
- **Hero**: Full-bleed aerial photograph of Miami/South Florida high-rise condos along the waterfront at golden hour or dusk. Overlay the headline and a subtle gradient. The image should feel aspirational — like looking down at a skyline full of buildings you manage.
- **Color palette**: Dark navy/charcoal base (#07070c) with warm gold accent (#c9a96e) and teal secondary (#2dd4bf). Light sections can use warm off-white (#f8f7f4).
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for headlines (like Cormorant Garamond or Playfair Display), clean sans-serif for body (like Plus Jakarta Sans or DM Sans). The contrast between serif headlines and sans body should feel like a luxury property brochure.
- **Photography**: Use real property images throughout — Miami high-rises, luxury condo lobbies, rooftop pools, maintenance crews at work, property managers on tablets. Every section should have a visual anchor.

## Design Rules
- Every section alternates between dark background and image/photo backgrounds
- NO section should be just text on a solid color without visual interest
- Use real photography — not illustrations or abstract shapes
- Gold (#c9a96e) is the primary accent for CTAs and highlights
- Teal (#2dd4bf) is the secondary accent for compliance/AI features
- Cards should have glass morphism or subtle frosted effects
- Generous whitespace — don't cram
- Mobile-first: everything should look great on iPhone 15 Pro
- Hover states: cards lift slightly, buttons glow, links underline
- Scroll animations: fade-up on section enter, parallax on photos
